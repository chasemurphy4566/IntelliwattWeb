History

Intelliwatt was founded in Calgary Alberta Canada on February 27th, 2024. On January 12th, due to a cold spell throughout the province, electrical usage was at an all-time high. This resulted in some of Albertas power grids to shut down. During the same day an alert was sent throughout Alberta suggesting Albertans reduce their electrical usage. Based on this scenario, our team found it important to recognize how much electricity we are using. This led us to build our proof of concept, an electrical monitoring system using IOT.
